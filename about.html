About Burnfeed

Most of my work includes using Web browsers and lot, and whichever one I use I cannot help but open a new tab and head to youtube.com or twitter.com, it is just a habit.

Thanks to a chrome extension called News Feed Eradicator, I was able to get rid of this bad habit by now making the entire feed hidden, each time I'd open a new tab, I'd see a blank feed and it just killed my habit.

Problem was, this extension was not available for Safari, and I was not able to find any other and so I decided to make one myself. And, here we are.

The reason why it has to exist on Safari is because if you have a Mac, Safari works the best and is very easy on the battery as well. I hope this small project is able to serve you well.
